Keep Car Cool Keep Car Cool Summer heat is hard on car interior. It can cause the headliner ceiling fabric to droop the padded dash to crack and upholstery to fade. Many summer related problems can be addressed with little common sense. Try to park in the shade. Avoid those wide open spaces that allow relentless full time exposure to the sun. Try to park near building to assure your car of some shade at least part of the time. Finally purchase sun shade. These inexpensive cardboard blockers placed in the windshield and rear windows help lower the interior temperature. More tips on protecting your car interior are in free pamphlet `HOW TO KEEP YOUR CAR LOOKING YOUNG published by the Car Care Council.

Some two dozen inmatesfrom the former Yugoslavia on April staged riot in Hungarianprison to demand the acceleration of their repatriation Hungarian andinternational media reported. National Prison Authority officials saidthe riot was initiated by three Bosnians. Later ethnic Albaniansfrom Kosovo joined the protest. They threatened to commit suicide andbroke windows and furniture to press their demands for repatriation. Theriot followed the March visit of Bosnian Embassy official who hadpromised the three Bosnians repatriation in few days. The three menwere arrested for illegally entering the country.
